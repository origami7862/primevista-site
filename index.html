About Us
PrimeVista Trading

PrimeVista Trading Co., Ltd. (卓景国际贸易有限责任公司) is a Shenzhen-based jewelry supplier specializing in premium stainless steel accessories for global brands and retailers.

Located in China's largest jewelry manufacturing hub, we offer direct factory pricing with flexible MOQs, sample orders, and complete white-label solutions for clients in the US, Europe, and Middle East markets.

Our Promise

Every piece is quality-checked before shipping. We use 316L stainless steel, genuine cubic zirconia, and PVD vacuum plating — no cheap spray coatings that wear off in weeks.
